How it works

Six steps to cashless travel

From registration to boarding — the whole journey takes less than 3 minutes.

1

Create account

Register with your name, email and phone number. Takes 60 seconds.

2

Choose company

Pick your transport company — SuperMetro, City Shuttle, Latema Travellers, and more.

3

Pick your route

Search or select your route. To and fro fares are set separately — you see exactly what you pay.

4

Choose subscription

Weekly or monthly. One price covers both directions — to and fro.

5

Pay via M-Pesa

A prompt appears on your phone. Enter your PIN. Done. Secure and instant.

6

Board with QR

Your QR ticket is ready immediately. Show it to the conductor — they scan and you board.
